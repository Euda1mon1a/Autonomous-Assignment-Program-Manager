## AT Demand by Rotation

Rotations that generate clinic attending need:

**High AT Demand:**
- FMC (residents in clinic daily)
- Procedures (some clinic time)

**Low AT Demand:**
- FMIT (inpatient, no clinic)
- Night Float (nights only)
- ER shifts (ER coverage)
- TDY rotations (off-site)
