## First Login

### Accessing the Application

1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge recommended)
2. Navigate to your organization's Residency Scheduler URL
3. You'll see the login page with the Residency Scheduler logo

### Logging In

1. Enter your username in the first field
2. Enter your password in the second field
3. Click the "Sign In" button

**First-time users:** Your administrator will provide your initial login credentials. You should change your password after your first login.

**Trouble logging in?**
- Make sure your Caps Lock is off
- Check that you're entering the correct username (it may be case-sensitive)
- Contact your program administrator if you've forgotten your password

### What Happens After Login

After successful login, you'll be automatically redirected to the Dashboard - your home page in the application.

## Dashboard Overview

The Dashboard is your command center. It provides a quick overview of important information and quick access to common tasks.

### Dashboard Layout

The Dashboard displays four main cards arranged in a grid:

#### 1. Schedule Summary Card
- Shows the current schedule status
- Displays today's date and current block information
- Quick access to view the full schedule
- May show recent schedule generation results

**What you'll see:**
- Current block dates
- Number of assignments
- Schedule generation status

#### 2. Compliance Alert Card
- Real-time ACGME compliance monitoring
- Shows any active violations or warnings
- Green checkmark when everything is compliant
- Warning indicators for issues requiring attention

**What to look for:**
- Green = All good, no violations
- Yellow/Orange = Warnings or minor issues
- Red = Critical violations requiring immediate attention

#### 3. Upcoming Absences Card
- Lists scheduled time off for the next few weeks
- Shows who is out and when
- Helps you plan coverage
- Click on an absence to view details

**Information displayed:**
- Person's name
- Absence type (vacation, medical, etc.)
- Start and end dates
- Number of days

#### 4. Quick Actions Card
- Shortcuts to common tasks
- Links to frequently used features
- Varies based on your role and permissions

**Common quick actions:**
- Generate Schedule
- Add Absence
- Add Person
- View Compliance Report

### Dashboard Best Practices

- **Check daily** - Start your day by reviewing the dashboard for new alerts or upcoming absences
- **Use quick actions** - Save time by using the quick action buttons instead of navigating through menus
- **Monitor compliance** - Keep an eye on the compliance card to catch issues early

## Navigation Basics

The navigation system helps you move between different areas of the application.

### Top Navigation Bar

Located at the top of every page, the navigation bar contains:

#### Left Side - Logo and Main Navigation
- **Residency Scheduler Logo** - Click to return to the Dashboard
- **Navigation Links** - Access different sections of the app

#### Right Side - User Menu
- **Your Name/Profile** - Access your profile and settings
- **Logout** - Sign out of the application

### Main Navigation Sections

The navigation menu includes these main sections (visibility depends on your role):

#### Dashboard
- **Icon:** Calendar
- **Purpose:** Your home page with overview and quick actions
- **Who can access:** Everyone

#### People
- **Icon:** Users
- **Purpose:** Manage residents and faculty profiles
- **Who can access:** Coordinators and Admins
- **What you can do:**
  - View all residents and faculty
  - Add new people
  - Edit existing profiles
  - Filter by PGY level or role
  - Export people data

#### Templates
- **Icon:** File/Document
- **Purpose:** Define rotation types and their rules
- **Who can access:** Coordinators and Admins
- **What you can do:**
  - Create rotation templates (Clinic, Inpatient, etc.)
  - Set capacity limits
  - Define supervision requirements
  - Specify specialty requirements

#### Absences
- **Icon:** Calendar with X
- **Purpose:** Manage time off and leave requests
- **Who can access:** Everyone (varying permissions)
- **What you can do:**
  - View absence calendar
  - Request time off
  - Edit or delete absence requests
  - Export absence data

#### Compliance
- **Icon:** Warning Triangle
- **Purpose:** Monitor ACGME compliance
- **Who can access:** Everyone (read-only for most)
- **What you can do:**
  - Check for ACGME violations
  - View compliance rules (80-hour, 1-in-7, supervision)
  - Review violation details
  - Navigate by month

#### Help
- **Icon:** Question Mark Circle
- **Purpose:** Access in-app help and reference materials
- **Who can access:** Everyone
- **What you'll find:**
  - Quick reference cards
  - Glossary of terms
  - Frequently asked questions
  - Print/save as PDF option

#### Settings
- **Icon:** Gear
- **Purpose:** Configure system settings
- **Who can access:** Admins only
- **What you can do:**
  - Set academic year dates
  - Configure ACGME rules
  - Choose default scheduling algorithm
  - Manage holidays

### Mobile Navigation

On smaller screens (phones and tablets), the navigation adapts:

- **Hamburger Menu** - Tap the three-line icon (☰) to open the navigation menu
- **Collapsible Menu** - Menu slides in from the side on mobile
- **Logo** - The app name may be shortened on small screens
- **Same Sections** - All the same sections are available, just reorganized

### Navigation Tips

- **Active Page Indicator** - The current page is highlighted in blue in the navigation
- **Keyboard Shortcuts** - Click links and buttons normally; standard browser shortcuts work
- **Breadcrumbs** - Page titles at the top help you know where you are
- **Back Button** - Use your browser's back button to return to previous pages

## User Profile Settings

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Look for your name in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
2. Click on your name to open the user menu
3. Select "Profile" to view your account information

### Profile Information

Your profile displays:
- Your full name
- Your role (Admin, Coordinator, Faculty, Resident)
- Your email address
- Account creation date

### Changing Your Password

**Note:** Password changes may need to be done by your administrator depending on your organization's security settings.

To request a password change:
1. Contact your program administrator
2. Provide them with your username
3. They can reset your password in the system

### Logging Out

To log out of the application:
1. Click your name in the top-right corner
2. Select "Logout" from the menu
3. You'll be returned to the login page

**Security tip:** Always log out when:
- Using a shared computer
- Stepping away from your desk for an extended period
- Finished using the application for the day

## Understanding Your Role

Your user role determines what you can see and do in the application:

### Admin Role
If you're an Admin, you have full access to:
- All pages and features
- Settings configuration
- User management
- All data editing capabilities
- Schedule generation

**Identifying mark:** You can see the Settings page in the navigation

### Coordinator Role
If you're a Coordinator, you can:
- View and manage schedules
- Add/edit people and absences
- Generate schedules
- View compliance reports
- Export data

**What you can't do:** Access the Settings page

### Faculty Role
If you're Faculty, you can:
- View schedules
- View people and absences
- View compliance reports

**What you can't do:** Edit data, generate schedules, or access settings

### Resident Role
If you're a Resident, you can:
- View your own schedule
- Request time off (absences)
- View your assignments

**What you can't do:** Most administrative functions

**Can't see something you need?** Contact your administrator to discuss your access level.

### Common Questions

#### "I can't find a feature I need"
- Check if your role has access to that feature
- Look in the Help page for feature locations
- Contact your administrator if you need additional permissions

#### "I made a mistake, how do I undo it?"
- Most edits can be reversed by editing the item again
- Deletions may not be reversible - the system will warn you first
- Contact your administrator if you need help correcting an error

#### "The page isn't loading or looks broken"
- Try refreshing your browser (F5 or Cmd+R)
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check your internet connection
- Contact your administrator if issues persist

#### "I see an error message"
- Read the error message carefully - it often explains the problem
- Common issues:
  - Required fields not filled in
  - Invalid date ranges
  - Network connectivity problems
- If the error persists, contact your administrator with details

## Browser Compatibility

Residency Scheduler works best with modern browsers:

### Recommended Browsers
- Google Chrome (version 90+)
- Mozilla Firefox (version 88+)
- Safari (version 14+)
- Microsoft Edge (version 90+)

### Mobile Browsers
- Safari on iOS
- Chrome on Android

**Note:** Internet Explorer is not supported. If you're using IE, please switch to a modern browser.

## System Requirements

To use Residency Scheduler, you need:

- **Internet Connection** - Active internet connection required
- **Modern Browser** - One of the browsers listed above
- **Screen Resolution** - 1024x768 or higher recommended
- **JavaScript Enabled** - The application requires JavaScript to function

**No installation required!** Residency Scheduler is a web application - just open your browser and log in.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

Standard web shortcuts work throughout the application:

- **Tab** - Move to next field or button
- **Shift+Tab** - Move to previous field or button
- **Enter** - Submit forms or activate buttons
- **Escape** - Close dialog boxes and modals
- **Ctrl/Cmd + F** - Find text on the current page
